MONSTER
CHALLENGE

Create a series of figures that can be collected and that have to live with a slime compound. Both figures and compound are boxed in a blind package that the user have to discover them. There is science experiment element to the design, encouraging the user to discover through different attempts.

Trend Research and Gameplay Design of this toy, targeted to boys 3-5 year.

Concept design and 3D Modelling of box and characters.

GAMEPLAY

1. OPEN THE LEAD

2. PUT THE HAND IN THE SLIME TO FIND THE RIGHT KEY.

3. OPEN THE MONSTER CHAMBER.

4. GRAB THE MONSTER FROM THE SLIME.

When the chamber is opened all the compound will fall on the monster.

It will be possible to put the ompound within the monster and play with it.
